In this level, you will be introduced to numbers and what comes after/before it. Here's the list of numbers in order:

Number Letter
0 Zero
1 One
2 Two
3 Three
4 Four
5 Five

These numbers are in order. For example, 7 comes after 6 and 6 comes before 7. 5 is the number between 4 and 6. These numbers also are ordered from small to big. For example,

3 is bigger than 4, 6 is smaller than 3, 7 and 8 is smaller than 10. Without further ado, let's begin the first exercise. Answer the question below correctly first!
