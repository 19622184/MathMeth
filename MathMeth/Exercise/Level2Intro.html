Let's begin with the addition! Here's an example:

4+3=7

As you can see, 4 added by 3 equals 7. The addition symbol means that the number on the left moves forward towards the number line 3 times

4, 5, 6, 7

It was shown that it takes 3 numbers for 4 to get to 7 and thus 4+3=7

Now let's see an example of subtraction! Here it is:

7-4=3

As you can see, 7 subtracted by 4 equals 3. The subtraction symbol means that the number on the left moves backward towards the number line 4 times

7, 6, 5, 4, 3

It was shown that it takes 4 numbers for 7 to get to 3 and thus 7-4=3
